About SARC

The Student Alumni Relations Cell (SARC) at NIT Agartala serves as a vital bridge connecting current students with our distinguished alumni community. Our mission is to foster meaningful relationships, facilitate knowledge transfer, and create opportunities for collaboration between generations of NITians.

Through various initiatives including mentorship programs, networking events, career guidance sessions, and cultural activities, SARC strengthens the bond between the institute and its alumni. We work tirelessly to preserve the legacy of NIT Agartala while building a vibrant community that supports the growth and success of both students and alumni.

Our Mission

To create a strong, engaged alumni network that supports student development and institutional growth.

Our Vision

Building lifelong connections between students, alumni, and the institute through meaningful engagement.

Our Work

Organizing alumni meets, mentorship programs, career guidance sessions, fundraising initiatives, and maintaining alumni database and communication channels.

About NIT Agartala

National Institute of Technology Agartala (NIT Agartala/NITA) is one of the prestigious institutions of national importance in India, established by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. Located in Jirania, Tripura, the institute has earned recognition for its academic excellence, innovative research, and strong industry connections.

Our Strengths

  • Academic Excellence: Comprehensive undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Science, and Management with a focus on quality education and research.
  • Research & Innovation: State-of-the-art laboratories and research facilities fostering cutting-edge research in various domains.
  • Industry Partnerships: Strong collaborations with leading companies ensuring excellent placement opportunities and industry exposure.
  • Infrastructure: Modern campus facilities including smart classrooms, well-equipped labs, digital library, sports complex, and comfortable hostel accommodations.
  • Cultural Diversity: A melting pot of students from across India, creating a rich multicultural environment.
  • Alumni Network: Successful alumni working in top organizations worldwide, contributing to various sectors and maintaining strong ties with the institute.
